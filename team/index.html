Our Team

Ramadhar Sarthi

President

Ramadhar Sarathi, President of Saans Foundation, is a social activist dedicated to combating human trafficking, promoting labor rights, and fostering equality. He leads awareness campaigns against exploitation and empowers youth in leadership. Through Saans Foundation, he works to bring positive societal change. His vision is to establish a just and prosperous society.

Anita Narang

Vice President

Anita Narang, a child trafficking survivor and social worker, is dedicated to bringing positive change in society. Her mission is to eliminate human trafficking and create a robust legal framework for workers and survivors. Through Saans Foundation, she empowers rural youth with leadership training and promotes social awareness to build a more inclusive and equitable society.

Dhananjay Khunte

Secretary

Dhanujay Khunte, co-founder of Saans Foundation, is a beacon of social change. As a survivor of child trafficking, he has become a powerful advocate against exploitation. With a background in English and Computer Applications, he leads initiatives in education, legal aid, and youth empowerment, driving positive transformation in marginalized communities.

Seema Kurre

Treasurer

Seema Kurre is the leader at Saans Foundation, dedicated to combating human and labor trafficking, empowering communities, and promoting education. With a focus on rural education and gender equality, she works to provide equal opportunities for all, ensuring a future of dignity and empowerment.

Khemlal Khaterji

Executive Director

Khemlal Khaterji, a child trafficking survivor and human rights activist, embodies values of equality and dignity. Through Saans Foundation, he empowers individuals through education and youth leadership training, focusing on holistic development. His vision is a society built on equality, respect, and sustainable change for future generations.

Anesh Kumari

Member

Anesh Kumari, leader of Saans Foundation, has been working for 2.5 years to combat human trafficking, empower communities, and promote education and leadership. With a focus on rural education, especially for girls, she believes education is key to building a society rooted in dignity, equality, and opportunity for all.
